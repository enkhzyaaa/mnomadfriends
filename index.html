About Mongolia

Mongolia, known as the "Land of Eternal Blue Sky," is a landlocked country nestled between Russia and China. With over 260 sunny days per year, Mongolia offers some of the world's most pristine wilderness and nomadic culture.

This vast nation is home to the legendary Gobi Desert, pristine lakes, rolling steppes, and the mighty Altai Mountains. Mongolia's rich history spans from the great Mongol Empire under Genghis Khan to its vibrant modern nomadic traditions.
